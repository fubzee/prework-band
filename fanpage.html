Fleetword Mac - An incredible journey!

Fleetwood Mac started in London in the sixties and enjoyed success in the UK and the United States with a number of one hits word wide. The band, like any other, saw a number of top artisits come and go and are known for rock, pop rock and British blues. During their career they sold more than 120 million records world wide and even have a a star on Hollywood's Walk of fame and received the Brit Award in recognition of the their outstanding contribution to music.
